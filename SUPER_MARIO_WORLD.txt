Super Mario World:
This game features you as mario and your goal is to work through various side scroller levels in order to advance to different map bosses. The ultimate goal of the game is to save Princess Toadstool from Bowser(the final boss). There are other later versions of this game that can be played on the Super Nintendo as well. They include Super Mario Bros, Super Mario Bros 2, and Super Mario Bros 3. They all feature different levels, bosses, characters, and abilities.
